Using Google Docs to Promote Student Collaboration in the Writing Process

In 2019, I presented at the IU 13 conference about instructional technologies. These materials are from a workshop on how to use the collaborative features in Google Docs to engage students in peer conversations about their writing. While I use these features with face-to-face students, they work well asyncronously and over distances. This was presented preCovid, but it seems especially relevant now.
