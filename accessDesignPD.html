Accessible Design: an introduction

With most instructional materials being presented in digital format this school year, students and teachers are struggling to do so much reading on an electronic screen. An introduction to accessible design covering the basics of font choice, contrast, and visual layout will help teachers to design assignments that reduce eye strain and clarify the content. This PD presents the basics of accessible design with time to explore resources that are most useful to K-12 educators.
